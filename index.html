Slapjack

Slapjack, also known as Slaps, is a card game of the matching family, generally played among children. It can often be a child's first introduction to playing cards. Here are the rules:

Note: If you have problems opening both players, the server will restart after about 6 minutes. This is because I set two sessions (one Websocket Connection) only, so for example, if there are already two pages and you refresh one, the server will restart.

  • 2 players required, a turn-based game and player1 will go first.

  • Click Deal button to play the game.

  • Shuffle a deck: Each player will get 26 cards randomly at the beginning.

  • Each player lifts one card at a time from their pile and places it face up in the center of the table.

  • When the card played to the center is a jack, the fun begins!

  • The first player to slap their hand down on the jack takes it, as well as all the cards beneath it.

  • The player winning these cards turns them face down, places them on their pile of cards.
